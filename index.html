Who We Are

At 12A+Education, we are passionate about providing a wide range of educational resources to students and educators.

Our collection includes books in Khmer, English, Chinese, Math, Physics, and Chemistry, catering to diverse learning needs.

Located in the heart of Phnom Penh, Cambodia, we are dedicated to promoting a love for learning and exploration.

Explore our extensive collection of educational books and resources, carefully curated to inspire and empower learners of all ages.

From captivating storybooks to in-depth study guides, we have something for every curious mind.

Immerse yourself in the world of literature, science, and mathematics with our engaging and informative selection of books.

Join us in our mission to make learning an exciting and enriching experience for everyone.
